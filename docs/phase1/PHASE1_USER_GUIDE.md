## FAQ

### Q: Why am I seeing "no benchmarks"?

**A:** Our database doesn't have enough comparable projects yet. We use synthetic benchmarks based on project quality. As more projects are scored, real benchmarks will appear.

### Q: Can benchmarks change?

**A:** Yes! As new projects are added to the database, average benchmarks may shift. Your score stays the same, but your percentile ranking can improve if you're ahead of new additions.

### Q: How is my industry determined?

**A:** Our AI analyzes your problem and solution text to extract industry automatically. If it's incorrect, that won't affect your score - it only improves our benchmark comparisons.

### Q: Are these benchmarks final?

**A:** These are v1.0 benchmarks. We continuously refine them as:

- More projects are added
- Historical outcomes are tracked
- Regional variations are understood

### Q: What if I'm in a different country?

**A:** Benchmarks pool projects globally by default. Future versions will offer regional breakdowns if that becomes a significant factor.
